Jame's favourite movies

These are my top 5 movies of all time (as of 2026)

1. Chainsaw Man - Reze Arc

This movie is such a masterpiece. The animation is stunning, the story is engaging and makes you fall in love with the characters.

2. Fate/Stay Night Heaven's Feel trilogy

Fantastic movie trilogy adaptation of the Heaven's Feel route. Animation is breathtaking, voice acting is immersive and makes you feel for the characters. Overall, peak cinema

3. I want to eat your pancreas

A touching and unique animated film that explores themes of love, loss, and acceptance. The animation is beautiful and the story is heartwarming.

4. Your Name

A visually stunning animated film that tells the story of two teenagers who mysteriously swap bodies. The animation is breathtaking and the story is emotionally resonant.

5. Josee, the Tiger and the Fish

A heartwarming and beautifully animated film that tells the story of a young girl and her relationship with a boy that shows her the wonders of what the outside can offer after growing up isolated due to her disability.
